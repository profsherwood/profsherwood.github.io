Transc. Mark DiMauro

Reading recorded by Richard Wirtz Emerson, 9 Ridge Road, Rutherford, NJ. Probably broadcast in Voices, WOSU, Columbus. October 7, 1950

This is just to say I have eaten the plums that were in the ice box exhale and which you were probably saving for breakfast Forgive me they were delicious so sweet and so cold
